Privacy Policy For JustSayin

Our Privacy Policy: Note that this app is created as a hobby project as a process of learning software development. We only require an email, username, and password for Authentication and do not use any of the information provided for anything else

Overview

At JustSayin, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy provides detailed information on what data we collect, how it is used, and the limited circumstances under which it might be shared. Understanding this policy will help you make informed decisions about using our services.

What Information We Collect

  1. Personal Identification Information:
    • Account Setup: The only information we collect is what is necessary for the creation and management of your account on JustSayin. This includes your email address, username, and password.

How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Services: We use your information solely to manage your account, which allows you to access and interact with our app.

Sharing Your Information

  • No Sharing: We do not share your personal information with any third parties, except as required to comply with the law or necessary to protect our rights or the safety of our users.

Security of Your Information

  • Data Retention: We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide you with our services. If you delete your account, your information is also deleted from our systems.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Control: You can access and update your account information at any time through the app settings.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time, which will remove your personal information from our records.

Changes to This Policy

  • Updates: We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes to our information practices. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email (sent to the email address specified in your account) or by means of a notice on this app prior to the change becoming effective.

For any questions regarding this privacy policy or the security of your personal data, please contact us through the support options provided within the app. Your continued use of JustSayin signifies your agreement to this Privacy Policy and any updates.
